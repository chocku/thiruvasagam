Acho Pathu

அச்சோப் பத்து
Chapter 51 · Composed at Thillai (Chidambaram)
← Ch. 50 All Chapters

Acho Pathu (The Song of Wonder) is the final chapter of the Thiruvasagam, a twelve-verse cycle built on a single exclamation: "acho" (அச்சோ), a Tamil cry of marvel and amazement. Every verse names one form of the poet's unworthiness — striving alongside the spiritually blind, taking false roads for true ones, destined to drown in sensual delusion, spending half a life in darkness — and then names the grace that rescued him from it. Each verse closes with the same refrain: "the grace the Father showed me — who else could receive such? Oh wonder!" The structure is antiphonal: degradation answered by marvel, confession answered by astonishment. As the last word Manikkavasagar speaks in the Thiruvasagam, "acho" names both the impossibility of the grace received and the only adequate response to it.

1

முத்திநெறி அறியாத · மூர்க்கரொடு · முயல்வேனைப் · பத்திநெறி அறிவித்துப் · பழவினைகள் பாறும்வண்ணம் · சித்தமலம் அறுவித்துச் · சிவமாக்கி · எனைஆண்ட · அத்தனெனக்கருளியவாறு · ஆர்பெறுவார் · அச்சோவே

who know not the path of liberation · with the stubborn fools · I who strive alongside them · showing the path of devotion · in a way that ancient karmas would scatter · severing the impurity of mind · making (me) into Shiva itself · who claimed me · the Father — the grace he showed me · who else would receive such? · Oh wonder!

I, who strove alongside those too stubborn to know the path of liberation — he showed me the way of devotion, scattered my ancient karmas, severed the impurities of my mind, made me into Shiva itself, and took me as his own. The grace the Father showed me — who else could receive such? Oh wonder!
2

நெறியல்லா நெறிதன்னை · நெறியாக நினைவேனைச் · சிறுநெறிகள் சேராமே · திருவருளே சேரும்வண்ணம் · குறியொன்றும் இல்லாத · கூத்தன்தன் கூத்தையெனக்கு · அறியும்வண்ணம் · அருளியவாறு · ஆர்பெறுவார் · அச்சோவே

a path that is no path at all · I who thought it the true way · without drawing me into petty by-ways · in a way to reach sacred grace itself · without any mark or sign · the Dancer's own dance · in a way for me to know and understand · the grace he showed · who else would receive such? · Oh wonder!

I, who took a false road for the true way — he kept me from petty by-ways, led me to sacred grace, and revealed to me the dance of the Dancer who bears no mark or sign. The grace he showed me — who else could receive such? Oh wonder!
3

பொய்யெல்லாம் மெய்யென்று · புணர்முலையார் போகத்தே · மையலுறக் கடவேனை · மாளாமே காத்தருளி · தையலிடங் கொண்டபிரான் · தன்கழலே சேரும்வண்ணம் · ஐயன்எனக்கருளியவாறு · ஆர்பெறுவார் · அச்சோவே

taking all falsehood as truth · in the pleasure of women with full breasts · I destined to be utterly enthralled · without letting me perish, guarding with grace · the Lord who holds woman as half his body (Ardhanarishvara) · to reach his sacred feet · the Master — the grace he showed me · who else would receive such? · Oh wonder!

I, who took all falsehood for truth and was destined to drown in sensual pleasure — he guarded me from perishing and brought me to the sacred feet of the Lord who holds woman as half his own body. The grace the Master showed me — who else could receive such? Oh wonder!
4

மண்ணதனிற் பிறந்தெய்த்து · மாண்டுவிழக் கடவேனை · எண்ணமிலா அன்பருளி · எனையாண்டிட்டு · என்னையும்தன் · சுண்ணவெண்ணீறு அணிவித்துத் · தூய்நெறியே சேரும்வண்ணம் · அண்ணல்எனக்கருளியவாறு · ஆர்பெறுவார் · அச்சோவே

born weary upon this earth · I destined to fall and die · granting love beyond all measure · having claimed me · even me as his own · adorning (me) with his powdered white sacred ash · in a way to reach the pure path · the great Lord — the grace he showed me · who else would receive such? · Oh wonder!

I, born weary upon this earth, destined to fall and die — he granted me measureless love, drew me in as his own, adorned me with his white sacred ash, and set me upon the pure path. The grace the great Lord showed me — who else could receive such? Oh wonder!
5

பஞ்சாய அடிமடவார் · கடைக்கண்ணால் இடர்ப்பட்டு · நெஞ்சாய துயர்கூர · நிற்பேன் · உன் அருள்பெற்றேன் · உய்ஞ்சேன்நான் · உடையானே · அடியேனை வருகவென்று · அஞ்சேல்என்று · அருளியவாறு · ஆர்பெறுவார் · அச்சோவே

women with feet soft as cotton · afflicted by the corner of their eye · grief cutting deep into the heart · I would have stood helpless · I received your grace · I was saved · O my Lord and Owner · calling me your servant saying "Come!" · saying "Fear not!" · the grace you showed · who else would receive such? · Oh wonder!

I would have stood helpless, afflicted by the sidelong glance of women with cotton-soft feet, grief cutting to the heart — but I received your grace, my Lord, and was saved! You called me, your servant: "Come!" — and "Fear not!" The grace you showed me — who else could receive such? Oh wonder!
6

வெந்துவிழும் உடற்பிறவி · மெய்யென்று · வினைபெருக்கி · கொந்துகுழல் கோல்வளையார் · குவிமுலைமேல் வீழ்வேனைப் · பந்தமறுத்து · எனையாண்டு · பரிசற என் துரிசுமறுத்து · அந்தமெனக்கருளியவாறு · ஆர்பெறுவார் · அச்சோவே

this body-birth that burns and perishes · taking as truth · multiplying karma · women with clustering hair and beautiful bangles · I who would fall upon their rounded breasts · severing the bonds · claiming me · completely cutting away every blemish of mine · the Final One — the grace he showed me · who else would receive such? · Oh wonder!

I, who took this burning body-birth for truth and multiplied my karmas, destined to fall upon the rounded breasts of bangle-clad women — he severed my bonds, made me his own, and cut away every last blemish. The grace the Final One showed me — who else could receive such? Oh wonder!
7

தையலார் மையலிலே · தாழ்ந்துவிழக் கடவேனைப் · பையவே கொடுபோந்து · பாசமெனும் தாழுருவி · உய்யும்நெறி காட்டுவித்திட்டு · ஓங்காரத்துட்பொருளை · ஐயன்எனக்கருளியவாறு · ஆர்பெறுவார் · அச்சோவே

in the delusion of women · I destined to sink and fall · gently leading me away · drawing out the lock called bondage (paasam) · causing me to be shown the path of liberation · the inner meaning of Om (Omkara) · the Master — the grace he showed me · who else would receive such? · Oh wonder!

I, destined to sink and fall in delusion — he gently led me away, drew out the lock of attachment from my soul, revealed the path of liberation, and disclosed the inner meaning of Om. The grace the Master showed me — who else could receive such? Oh wonder!
8

சாதல்பிறப்பென்னும் · தடஞ்சுழியில் தடுமாறி · காதலின்மிக்க · அணியிழையார் · கலவியிலே விழுவேனை · மாதொருகூறுடையபிரான் · தன்கழலே சேரும்வண்ணம் · ஆதியெனக்கருளியவாறு · ஆர்பெறுவார் · அச்சோவே

what is called death and birth · floundering in the great whirlpool · filled with much desire · jeweled women · I who would fall into union with them · the Lord who holds woman as one portion of his form (Ardhanarishvara) · to reach his sacred feet · the Primordial One — the grace he showed me · who else would receive such? · Oh wonder!

I, floundering in the great whirlpool of birth and death, destined to fall in desire into the embrace of jeweled women — the Primordial One, the Lord who holds woman as half his own form, brought me to reach his sacred feet. The grace the Primordial One showed me — who else could receive such? Oh wonder!
9

செம்மைநலம் அறியாத · சிதடரொடும் திரிவேனை · மும்மைமலம் அறுவித்து · முதலாய முதல்வன்தான் · நம்மையும் ஓர்பொருளாக்கி · நாய்சிவிகை ஏற்றுவித்த · அம்மையெனக்கருளியவாறு · ஆர்பெறுவார் · அச்சோவே

who know not the goodness of righteousness · with the blind ones I who wandered · severing the three impurities (anava, karma, maya) · the First among firsts — the Primordial Source · making even us into something of worth · mounting this dog in a palanquin · the Mother — the grace shown to me · who else would receive such? · Oh wonder!

I, who wandered with those blind to all righteousness — the Primordial Source severed the three impurities from me, made even me into something of worth, and mounted this dog in a palanquin. The grace the Mother showed me — who else could receive such? Oh wonder!
10

செத்திடமும் · பிறந்திடமும் · இனிச்சாவாதிருந்திடமும் · அத்தனையும் அறியாதார் · அறியும்அறிவு எவ்வறிவோ · ஒத்தநிலம் ஒத்தபொருள் · ஒருபொருளாய் பெரும்பயனை · அத்தன்எனக்கருளியவாறு · ஆர்பெறுவார் · அச்சோவே

the place of dying · the place of birth · the deathless place where one need never die again · those who know none of these · what kind of knowing is knowledge at all? · equal ground, equal substance · as one substance — the great fruit · the Father — the grace he showed me · who else would receive such? · Oh wonder!

The place of dying, the place of birth, the deathless place where one need never die again — for those who know none of these, what kind of knowing is knowledge at all? On equal ground, of equal substance — the one great fruit: the grace the Father showed me — who else could receive such? Oh wonder!
11

படியதினிற் கிடந்து · இந்தப் பசு பாசம் தவிர்ந்துவிடும் · குடிமையிலே திரிந்து · அடியேன் · கும்பியிலே விழாவண்ணம் · நெடியவனும் நான்முகனும் · நீர்கான்றும் காணவொண்ணா · அடிகளெனக்கருளியவாறு · ஆர்பெறுவார் · அச்சோவே

lying here in this world · about to be freed from this bond of soul (pasu-pasam) · wandering in lowliness · I your servant · in a way to not fall into the abyss of hell · even Vishnu the tall one and Brahma the four-faced · even searching the cosmic waters could not behold · those sacred feet — the grace they showed me · who else would receive such? · Oh wonder!

Lying in this world, about to be freed from the bond of soul, wandering in lowliness — he kept me, your servant, from falling into the abyss of hell. Those sacred feet that even Vishnu and Brahma could not behold though they searched the cosmic waters — the grace those feet showed me — who else could receive such? Oh wonder!
12

பாதியெனும் இரவுதங்கி · பகல் எமக்கு இரைதேடி · வேதனையில் அகப்பட்டு · வெந்துவிழக் கடவேனை · சாதிகுலம் பிறப்பறுத்து · சகமறிய வெனையாண்ட · ஆதியெனக்கருளியவாறு · ஆர்பெறுவார் · அச்சோவே

spending half of life as if in night · in what we call day seeking sustenance · caught in suffering and torment · I destined to burn and fall · cutting through caste and lineage · for the whole world to see, claiming me · the Primordial One — the grace he showed me · who else would receive such? · Oh wonder!

I, spending half my life lost in darkness, searching for sustenance in what we call day, caught in torment, destined to burn and fall — he cut through caste and lineage, and enrolled me as his own for the whole world to see. The grace the Primordial One showed me — who else could receive such? Oh wonder!
