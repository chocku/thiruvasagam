Thiruvasagam · Sacred Utterances of Manikkavasagar

A classical Tamil devotional text composed by the poet-saint Manikkavasagar, addressed to Lord Shiva. Among the most revered works in Tamil Shaiva literature, it comprises 51 hymns of intense personal devotion and mystical insight.
