திருவெண்பா — THIRUVENBA
=========================
By Manikkavasagar | Thiruvasagam, Chapter 47
Composed at Thiruperundurai
(அணைந்தோர் தன்மை — The Nature of Those Who Have Attained)

Thiruvenba (The Sacred Venba Verses) is a chapter of ten short venba-metre poems, each a flash of devotional insight. The subtitle calls these verses the nature of those who have truly attained union. Manikkavasagar alternates between bewilderment at his own condition ("what shall I do?") and awe at what the Lord of Perunthurai has accomplished in him — entering his heart, severing karma, granting nectar, becoming medicine. The venba metre traditionally ends with a short conclusive line; in each verse that final line lands the theological point with quiet force.
