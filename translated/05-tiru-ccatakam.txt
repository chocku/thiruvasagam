திருச்சதகம் — THIRUCHATHAKAM
====================================
By Manikkavasagar | Thiruvasagam, Chapter 5
Composed at Thiruperundurai

A hundred verses tracing ten inner stages of the soul’s ascent: from Realization of Truth (mey uNardhal) through Instruction, Piercing the Veil, Self-Purification, Reciprocal Exchange, Experiential Purity, Lament of Compassion, Weeping in Bliss, Supreme Bliss, to Transcendent Bliss (Ananda Adheeda) — Manikkavasagar pours out his unworthiness, his surrender, and his ultimate dissolution into Shiva at Thiruperundurai.

**I must sing you — Praise! Singing, languishing, dwelling in you, melting, dissolving and dissolving — I must dance — Praise! This dog I am, in the cosmic stage, at the flowers of your dancing anklets — I must join — Praise! Remove from me this worm-cage — Praise! All falsehood must be liberated — Praise! Grant liberation and grace — Praise — O True One among your truth-filled ones!**
