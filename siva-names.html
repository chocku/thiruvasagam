Names of Siva in Thiruvasagam

திருவாசகம் · சிவ நாமங்கள்
35 names and epithets · 5 thematic categories

Thiruvasagam — the Sacred Utterances — is the ninth-century Tamil devotional masterwork of the poet-saint Manikkavasagar, comprising 51 hymns addressed to Lord Siva at Chidambaram and beyond. Across its opening decade of verses alone, Manikkavasagar calls upon Siva by dozens of names, each one a compressed theology: cosmic sovereign, silent teacher, wandering ascetic, intimate father, and the stainless ground of being itself. These epithets are not decorative — each encodes a distinct relationship between the devotee and the divine, moving from Siva's transcendent attributes down to the intensely personal ("my Mother," "my beloved"). The table below gathers all 35 names found in the text, arranged by thematic category, so that any name can be linked directly and explored in its verse context.
